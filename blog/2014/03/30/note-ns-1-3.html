Define of computer security

  • Confidentiality:

    • Data confidentiality: Private information is not available to unauthorized individuals.
    • Privacy: control what information related to them may be collected, stored, by whom or to whom that the information may be disclosed.

  • Integrity:

    • Data integrity: data only changed in a specified and authorized manner.
    • System integrity: system function is in an intended and unimpaired manner, free from deliberate or inadvertent unauthorized manipulation of the system.

  • Availability: systems work promptly and service is not denied to authorized users.
